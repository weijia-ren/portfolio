## Interview Types at a Glance

| Type | What they assess | Prep strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Behavioral (BQ)** | Past behavior predicts future behavior | STAR stories mapped to competencies |
| **Product Sense / Case** | Structured thinking about product problems | Frameworks + practice out loud |
| **Technical** | Statistical knowledge, analytical reasoning | Methods reference + worked examples |
| **Portfolio / Case Study** | Depth of past work, communication | 2–3 polished stories with visuals |
| **Cross-Functional** | Collaboration, influence, conflict resolution | Stories showing stakeholder management |
